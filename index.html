About Me

Bio

Jamil Matheny moved to Richmond, VA from Elizabeth, New Jersey. He studied Information Technology at Ashford University and proceeded to work in a fast-paced environment, ensuring his clients gain a quality product. Passionate about photography and web developing, he became fascinated with it since high school and made it into his profession. In his free time, he loves to go out venturing with his lovely wife and son. He also produces music for local artists in his area and has lead three local bands as a vocalist in the Richmond area. He is committed to his work as a web developer and a photographer to deliver excellent work to the viewer's eye.
